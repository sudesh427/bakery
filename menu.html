pound cake

Pound cake is a type of cake traditionally made with a pound of each of four ingredients:flour,butter,eggs,and sugar.Pound cakes are generally baked in either a loaf pan or a Bundt mold,and served either dusted with powdered sugar,lightly glazed,or sometimes with a coat of icing.

chocolate cake

Chocolate cake or chocolate gateau is a cake flavored with melted chocolate,cocoa powder,or both.

butter cake

A butter cake is a cake in which one of the main ingredients is butter.Butter cake is baked with basic ingredients:butter,sugar,eggs,flour,and leavening agents such as baking powder or baking soda.

sponge cake

Any recipe that contains no baking soda or baking powder but lots of whipped eggs or egg whites.
